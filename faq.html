Below the most frequently asked questions are asked. Click on the question card to view the answer.

Who made this thermostat interface?

This web interface for a thermostat was created by group 26, consisting of Kas Burgers, Rodger van der Heijden and Vlad-Andrei Popescu.

How can I add another switch?

You can add another switch by going to the menu and going to the "Switches" page. There you can create a switch for all days you want.

How can I permanently override the temperature, for instance when I go on holiday?

To make sure you do not unnecessarily heat your home when you're not there, change the temperature on the home screen and select the toggle button beneath it.

Why can't I select temperatures below 5 degrees or above 30 degrees?

Because the assignment specified so.
