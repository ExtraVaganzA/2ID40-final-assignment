How can I turn on the vacation mode?

The vacation mode can be found by clicking on the sub-menu "settings" in the navigation bar.
You will be led to a page where the first option is the vacation mode.

Who made this thermostat interface?

This web interface for a thermostat was created by group 26, consisting of Kas Burgers, Rodger van der Heijden and Vlad-Andrei Popescu.

Where can I change the units to imperial or metric?

The units that you will see can be changed via the settings. There you can indicate (among other settings) whether you prefer an imperial unit scale or a metric unit scale.
